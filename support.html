AloGo Support

Use this page for account help, login issues, driver/rider support, safety concerns, and data or deletion requests.

Account and OTP

If you cannot receive an OTP code, check your phone number, network connection, and try again after a short wait.

Trips and drivers

For ride, driver availability, or map issues, include your phone number, approximate time, and a short description of the issue.

Privacy requests

You may request account deletion or data help. We may verify ownership before processing security-sensitive requests.

Safety

For urgent safety issues, contact local emergency services first, then report the issue to AloGo support.

Contact AloGo

Email: support@alogo.online

If email is not yet active, use this address as the official support contact while the mailbox is being configured.
