Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how AloGo collects, uses, and protects information when you use our mobile application and website.

Information we collect

We may collect account information, phone number verification data, profile details, vehicle information for drivers, trip activity, messages, device information, and support requests.

Location data

AloGo uses location data to show nearby drivers, help riders request trips, support active trips, and allow online drivers to appear on the live map. Background location may be used only for online drivers and active trips.

Notifications and communication

We may use push notifications for ride updates, driver availability, messages, and important account or safety information.

Service providers

AloGo may use trusted providers such as Supabase for authentication and data storage, Google Maps for maps and places, and monitoring tools such as Sentry to diagnose crashes and improve reliability.

How we protect data

We use access controls, platform security, limited data collection, and production safeguards. Sensitive server secrets are not stored inside the mobile app.

Your choices

You can control location and notification permissions from your device settings. You may request account deletion or data support by contacting us through the support page.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact AloGo support through our support page.
